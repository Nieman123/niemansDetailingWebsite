Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions most clients ask before booking.

What do I need to provide for my appointment?

For a full detail, you must provide both electrical access and water access at the service location. For interior-only service, electrical access only is required.

Do I need to be present for the full appointment?

No, I just need access to the vehicle, electrical access, and water when I arrive.

How long does a service usually take?

Timing depends on package, vehicle size, and condition. I can give a better estimate when you book, but expect anywhere from 3 to 5 hours for a full detail.

What if weather is bad on appointment day?

If weather will affect service quality, I will contact you and coordinate the next available time.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online through the schedule page, request a quote, or call/text directly at (828) 273-3894.

What kinds of payment do you take?

Cash is preferred, but I also accept Venmo, PayPal, and Zelle.

Need something specific not listed here? Call or text and I will walk you through it.
