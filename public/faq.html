Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions most clients ask before booking.

What do I need to provide for my appointment?

For a full detail, you must provide both electrical access and water access at the service location. For interior-only service, electrical access only is required.

Do I need to be present for the full appointment?

Not the whole time. We just need access to the vehicle when we arrive and when the service is complete.

How long does a service usually take?

Timing depends on package, vehicle size, and condition. Full details typically take longer than maintenance packages. We can give a better estimate when you book.

What if weather is bad on appointment day?

If weather will affect service quality or safety, we will contact you and coordinate the next available time.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online through the schedule page, request a quote, or call/text directly at (828) 273-3894.
