About Me

I decided to start Nieman's Detailing simply because I love cars. I love my car and my customers love theirs just as much. My goal is to have every customer trust that their car was treated with the utmost care and respect, so they can have peace of mind and create more value from their hard earned money. So many other "detailing" services skip corners, hide dirt, and use harsh chemicals in sake of quick car turnover. A detail needs to be a timely, conscientious, and safe process to avoid unnecessary or irreparable damage to a vehicle’s paint or finish.

When I'm not detailing you might find me in the forest riding my bike, or hanging out with my cats at home. I also help local businesses with their websites, and I'm developing an Unreal Engine 4 based video game in my free time.
If you're a mountain biker in the area, get in contact! I'm looking for new people to ride with. Some of my favorite places to ride are Big Ivy, Kitsuma, and my own backyard.
