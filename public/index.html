The Works

This package is a safe and comprehensive cleaning of both the interior and exterior including wheel, tire, and engine bay detailing, steam cleaning, a premium wax and more.

Best for first-time clients or vehicles that have gone a while between details. Most appointments run 3-4 hours depending on vehicle size and overall condition.

Why it may cost more: pricing varies by vehicle size and condition. Large SUVs/trucks, heavy soil, pet hair, excessive sap/bug buildup, and add-ons like clay/iron decontamination or paint correction increase labor and materials. We’ll quote it up front so there are no surprises.

Service Details >

$300 sedan / $350 SUV

Quick Once Over

A quicker, less intensive detail that removes the majority of the dirt and contaminants on the interior and exterior. Wheels and glass are cleaned and the interior is vacuumed and wiped down. Finished with a sun-safe SiO₂ spray sealant for slickness and water beading.

Great for regular upkeep between full details and ideal every 4-8 weeks to keep your vehicle looking clean without committing to a full restoration each visit.

Service Details >

$200 sedan / $250 SUV

Monthly Mini

A 60–75 minute maintenance service for existing clients: exterior wash, wheels/tires, glass, light interior vacuum and wipe-down, finished with a SiO₂ spray sealant for fresh gloss and hydrophobics.

Designed as a maintenance plan after an initial detail so buildup never gets out of hand and your protection stays topped up month to month.

Paint Correction and Polishing

This process removes scratches, swirls, holograms and oxidation defects in the clear-coat of your vehicle resulting in an extremely glossy, mirror-like finish. The ultimate restoration of your paint

Single-step and multi-step correction options are available based on paint condition and your target finish. A test spot helps determine the safest and most effective approach.

Metal Polishing

From Airstreams to truck wheels to boats, if it's metal I can polish it. Put the reflections back into your metal and protect it for the long run.

Aluminum, chrome, and stainless can all be restored. Time and final pricing depend on oxidation level, access, and the amount of correction needed.

Trim Restoration

Permanently restore the grey UV damaged plastics on your vehicle to the deep rich black finish that came from the factory.

Unlike temporary dressings, this process deep-cleans and restores trim for a longer-lasting, factory-like appearance that resists wash-off and fading.

Service Details >

$75

New Car Decontamination and Protection

The best way to protect the finish of your new purchase. Have the iron and industrial fallout contamination removed and the paint sealed with long lasting and protective wax. Also included is an interior and exterior trim protectant to block damaging UV rays. Ensure that your paint and plastic remain how it looked from the factory. Paint correction can be added additionally.

Even new vehicles often arrive with embedded rail dust and dealer-installed residue. Early decontamination and protection keeps the finish cleaner, glossier, and easier to maintain.

Service Details >

$125

Overspray Removal

A complete removal of excess paint or other substance that's been sprayed onto your paint. Whether it's spray foam insulation, or paint from the body shop, this process leaves the paint clean and smooth to the touch.

Each job starts with a test spot so the safest method is used for your specific contamination. Goal is full removal while protecting your clear coat.

Hard Water Spot Removal

Water spots on your paint or glass? Get them removed permanently!

Mineral deposits can etch into paint and glass if left untreated. This service removes buildup and restores clarity and smoothness.

Odor Removal

Sometimes a vehicle can develop an unwanted smell or even mold over in humid environments like the Appalachian mountains. Our industrial strength ozone and steam treatment will leave your car smelling better.

Source removal comes first, then ozone treatment for deeper neutralization. Smoke, mildew, and pet odors may require multiple steps depending on severity.

About Me

I decided to start Nieman's Detailing simply because I love cars. I love my car and my customers love theirs just as much. My goal is to have every customer trust that their car was treated with the utmost care and respect, so they can have peace of mind and create more value from their hard earned money. So many other "detailing" services skip corners, hide dirt, and use harsh chemicals in sake of quick car turnover. A detail needs to be a timely, conscientious, and safe process to avoid unnecessary or irreparable damage to a vehicle’s paint or finish.

When I'm not detailing you might find me in the forest riding my bike, or hanging out with my cats at home. I also help local businesses with their websites, and I'm developing an Unreal Engine 4 based video game in my free time.
If you're a mountain biker in the area, get in contact! I'm looking for new people to ride with. Some of my favorite places to ride are Big Ivy, Kitsuma, and my own backyard.
