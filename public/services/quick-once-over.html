Quick Once Over

Typical price: $125 sedan / $150 SUVIdeal cadence: every 4-8 weeks

What This Service Covers

Quick Once Over is a streamlined interior and exterior mobile detail focused on removing most day-to-day dirt and road film without the time commitment of a full restoration package. The service includes a careful hand wash, wheels and glass cleaning, interior vacuum and wipe-down, and a SiO2 spray sealant that boosts gloss, slickness, and water behavior for easy upkeep between deeper details.

When You Might Need It

This option works best for vehicles that are already in decent condition and just need regular maintenance before buildup becomes stubborn. If you drive daily, park outside, carry pets or kids, or commute through Asheville weather and mountain roads, booking this service every month or two helps prevent interior grime and exterior contamination from turning into a much bigger correction job.

Why It Is Worth Doing

Routine maintenance detailing usually costs less over time than waiting until a vehicle needs heavy corrective work, because less contamination has a chance to bond to paint, plastics, and glass. Keeping a vehicle on a Quick Once Over schedule preserves appearance, supports resale value, and makes each future visit faster since the car stays in a maintained condition instead of cycling between very clean and very neglected.
