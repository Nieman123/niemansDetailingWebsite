Quick Once Over - Maintenance Detail

Typical price: $200 sedan / $250 SUVTypical time: 2-2.5 hoursIdeal cadence: every 4-8 weeks

What This Service Covers

Quick Once Over is a streamlined interior and exterior mobile detail focused on removing most day-to-day dirt and road film without the time commitment of a full restoration package. The service includes a careful hand wash, wheels and glass cleaning, interior vacuum and wipe-down, and a SiO2 spray sealant that boosts gloss, slickness, and water behavior for easy upkeep between deeper details in Asheville and surrounding areas.

When You Might Need It

This option works best for vehicles that are already in decent condition and just need regular maintenance before buildup becomes stubborn. If you drive daily, park outside, carry pets or kids, or commute through Asheville weather and mountain roads, booking this service every month or two helps prevent interior grime and exterior contamination from turning into a much bigger correction job.

Why It Is Worth Doing

Routine maintenance detailing usually costs less over time than waiting until a vehicle needs heavy corrective work, because less contamination has a chance to bond to paint, plastics, and glass. Keeping a vehicle on a Quick Once Over schedule preserves appearance, supports resale value, and makes each future visit faster since the car stays in a maintained condition instead of cycling between very clean and very neglected.

Our Process

  1. Wheels & Tires: Deep cleaning of wheel faces and tire sidewalls.
  2. Hand Wash: Gentle mitt wash to remove road film without scratching.
  3. Spray Sealant: Application of SiO2 protection for gloss and water beading.
  4. Interior: Thorough vacuum and wipe down of all hard surfaces.

Common Questions

Does this remove stains?
No, this is a maintenance service. Stains require a full detail or spot treatment.

How often should I get this?
We recommend every 4-8 weeks to keep your car protected and easy to clean.
