Monthly Mini - Recurring Detail

Typical price: $85 sedan / $100 SUVTypical time: 60-75 minutes

What This Service Covers

Monthly Mini is a maintenance-focused detail for existing clients in the Asheville area that includes an exterior wash, wheel and tire cleaning, glass cleaning, light interior vacuuming, interior wipe-down, and a SiO2 spray sealant refresh. It is meant to keep a professionally detailed vehicle in clean condition without repeating deep-correction labor on every visit.

When You Might Need It

This service is best used after an initial detail when you want to maintain consistent results month to month, especially if the vehicle is a daily driver or regularly exposed to pollen, rain, or mountain road dust. A predictable monthly cadence is useful for busy households because the car stays presentable year-round instead of needing occasional emergency cleanups before important events or trips.

Why It Is Worth Doing

Regular monthly detailing reduces long-term wear by preventing contamination layers from setting into clear coat, trim, carpets, and touch surfaces, which protects both appearance and value. It also keeps protective products active and helps avoid larger corrective appointments, making Monthly Mini one of the most practical ways to control detailing costs while enjoying a consistently clean vehicle.

Our Process

  1. Maintenance Wash: Careful hand wash to maintain swirl-free paint.
  2. Wheel Face Clean: Regular removal of corrosive brake dust.
  3. Interior Touch-Up: Light vacuum and dusting to keep the cabin fresh.
  4. Top-Up Protection: Boost existing wax/coating with a spray detailer.

Common Questions

Can I book this as a first-time client?
No, this particular service is exclusively for vehicles we have already detailed to maintain that standard.

What if my car is very dirty?
If it has been months since your last clean, we may suggest a Quick Once Over instead to properly reset the vehicle.
