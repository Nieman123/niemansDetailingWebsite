The Works - Full Auto Detail

Typical price: $300 sedan / $350 SUVTypical time: 4-6 hours

What This Service Covers

The Works is the most complete interior and exterior detail package from Nieman's Detailing, designed to reset a vehicle in one appointment with deep interior cleaning, wheel and tire care, engine bay detailing, careful paint-safe washing, and premium paint protection. It is built for drivers in Asheville, Weaverville, and Hendersonville who want one thorough mobile detailing visit that handles heavy buildup and leaves the vehicle looking, feeling, and smelling dramatically cleaner.

When You Might Need The Works

This package is usually the right choice when it has been several months since the last professional detail, after road trips or seasonal weather has added grime and contamination, or before selling or trading in the vehicle. It is also the best starting point for first-time clients, because beginning with a full detail makes future maintenance washes faster, more affordable, and more effective.

Why It Is Worth Doing

A full mobile detail protects long-term vehicle value by reducing paint wear, slowing UV and contamination damage, and keeping interior materials from drying, staining, or holding odors. Beyond appearance, consistent full detailing improves comfort and pride of ownership, helps preserve resale value, and gives you a clean baseline so future detailing and protection services perform better.
