Headlight Restoration

Typical price: $75Improves nighttime visibility

What This Service Covers

Headlight restoration removes oxidation, yellowing, and surface haze from plastic headlight lenses through controlled sanding and polishing stages followed by a UV-resistant finishing layer. The goal is to recover optical clarity and a factory-like look without replacing the entire headlight assembly, which can be much more expensive.

When You Might Need It

You should consider this service when headlights look cloudy, appear dull in daylight, or throw weak and scattered light on the road at night. If you notice reduced forward visibility in rain, a faded front-end appearance, or if your vehicle is getting ready for sale or inspection, restoration is a practical fix before lens deterioration worsens.

Why It Is Worth Doing

Clear headlights improve driving safety by directing more usable light where you need it, helping reaction time on dark roads and in poor weather. Restoration also updates the look of the entire front end, supports resale presentation, and can delay the cost of full headlight replacement while preserving the original components.
