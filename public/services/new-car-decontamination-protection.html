What This Service Covers

This service removes embedded contaminants like rail dust, industrial fallout, and dealer-installed residues that often remain on "new" vehicles after transport and lot storage, then applies durable paint and trim protection. The process is designed to preserve the fresh look of your new purchase in Asheville while creating a cleaner and better-protected baseline from day one.

When You Might Need It

The best time is early ownership, ideally within the first few weeks, before contamination has more time to bond and before routine washing adds avoidable marring. It is especially useful if your new vehicle has been transported long distances, sat on a dealer lot for months, or will be parked outside through heavy sun and changing mountain weather.

Why It Is Worth Doing

Early decontamination and protection helps maintain gloss, makes routine washing easier, and reduces the chance that contaminants etch into paint or stain trim over time. Starting with professional protection also simplifies your long-term maintenance strategy, because future cleaning is less labor-intensive and the finish keeps a newer appearance for longer.

Our Process

  1. Strip Wash: We remove dealer glazes and shipping oils.
  2. Iron Decon: Chemical removal of embedded rail dust from transport.
  3. Clay Bar: Physical removal of any remaining surface fallout.
  4. Sealant: Application of long-term synthetic protection.

Common Questions

My car is brand new, does it really need this?
Yes! Factory protection is minimal, and rail dust from transport is very common and can rust if left on the paint.

Is this a ceramic coating?
This package includes a durable sealant (6+ months). We can upgrade you to a multi-year ceramic coating upon request.
